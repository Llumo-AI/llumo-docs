# Create Your Account

### Step 1
Visit [https://www.llumo.ai/](https://www.llumo.ai/) using your preferred browser.

### Step 2
Click on **"Get Started"**. You can sign up using your Google account or enter your company email.

### Step 3
Create a secure password. Make sure it includes at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (like `!`, `@`, or `#`).

Once you're signed up, you’ll see a welcome form.

### Step 4 
Fill in the following details to get started:
- **Name**: Enter your name
- **Company Website**: Add your company’s website URL
- **Monthly LLM Spend**: Select your monthly LLM spend from the dropdown list.
- **What are you looking for?**: Let us know what solution or help you're looking for.

Finally, click the **"Submit"** button, and you're all set.

### Step 5
To get started quickly and make the most of LLUMO, **book an onboarding call** with our team. We'll guide you through the platform, answer your questions, and ensure you’re set up for success.  
Click **"Book an Onboarding Call"** below to pick a time that works best for you!  
We’re excited to help you get started!
